What are Recreational Nuclear Reactors?

Recreational nuclear reactors are small-scale reactors designed for educational and research purposes, providing a safe and controlled environment for learning about nuclear energy.

Benefits

  • Safe and controlled environment for education.
  • Promotes understanding of nuclear technology.
  • Supports research and development in nuclear science.

Safety Measures

All recreational nuclear reactors are designed with multiple safety systems to ensure the protection of users and the environment. Regular inspections and maintenance are conducted to uphold safety standards.
